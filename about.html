Welcome to the Bright Student Portal

The Bright Student Portal is designed to provide students with easy access to important information and resources. Our aim is to streamline the student experience by offering a centralized platform for managing academic schedules, notices, and leave requests.

Features

  • View upcoming deadlines and events
  • Submit and track leave requests
  • Access the noticeboard for important announcements
  • Integrate Google Calendar for better time management
